5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where We Store Your Data

Cloud Storage:

  • User data is primarily stored on Google Cloud Platform via Firebase Firestore (United States)
  • Images and media files are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Payment data is stored by Stripe in accordance with PCI DSS standards

Local Device Storage:

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using Expo Secure Store (encrypted)
  • Temporary cache data may be stored locally
  • No payment information is stored on your device

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of authentication tokens on your device
  • Password hashing via Firebase Authentication

Access Controls:

  • User-scoped access control in Firestore (you can only access your own data)
  • Organization-based permissions for team features
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID) for sensitive features like journal access

Authentication:

  • JWT token-based session management
  • Automatic token refresh every 5 minutes
  • Secure logout that clears local credentials

Monitoring:

  • Regular security reviews of Firebase security rules
  • Error monitoring and logging (when Sentry is enabled)

Despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

Active Accounts:

  • Your account information and user-generated content are retained for as long as your account is active

Chat History:

  • Subscriber chat history is retained until you manually clear it
  • Free user chat history is stored temporarily and cleared upon logout

Journal Entries:

  • Retained until you manually delete them (subscriber feature)

Confessional Sessions:

  • Retained until you manually clear the session

Usage Analytics:

  • Aggregate analytics data is retained indefinitely for service improvement
  • Individual session data is retained for up to 2 years

Payment Records:

  • Transaction history is retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes

Deleted Accounts:

  • Upon account deletion request, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, dispute resolution)

6.2 Deleting Your Data

You have the right to delete your data:

In-App Controls:

  • Clear chat history via the chat interface
  • Clear confessional sessions via the confessional interface
  • Delete individual journal entries

Account Deletion:

  • To delete your entire account, please contact us at info@lysaratech.com
  • We will process your deletion request within 30 days
  • Some information may be retained as described in Section 6.1
