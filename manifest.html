About Manifest

Manifest Inventory Management eliminates coordination friction between field teams and warehouse operations through intelligent order generation, real-time tracking, and AI-assisted supply chain planning. Built for organizations managing multiple locations with distributed workforces.

Key Features

AI-Powered Ordering

Lana AI Assistant

Simply tell the system what you need in plain English. AI automatically identifies items, calculates costs, and creates orders.

AI BOM Builder

Upload project blueprints (PDFs) and watch AI extract items, quantities, and specs.

Voice Integration

Hands-free operation for busy warehouse environments with smart component-based matching.

Multi-Location Control

Branch Management

Manage 1 to unlimited warehouse locations with smart duplication to copy inventory setups instantly.

Real-Time Visibility

See stock levels across all locations at a glance with branch-specific reorder thresholds.

Complete Order Workflow

  • Easy field worker submission from mobile or desktop
  • Real-time status tracking
  • Prioritized warehouse queue management
  • Automated notifications

Smart Inventory Management

  • Three-level thresholds (Min/Par/Critical) with visual indicators
  • Auto-reorder alerts
  • QR code integration for fast scanning
  • Vehicle stock tracking with auto-replenishment

Supply Chain Mastery

  • Comprehensive distributor management
  • SKU mapping to distributor part numbers
  • Price tracking and monitoring
  • Automated purchase order generation
  • Receiving logs with discrepancy tracking

Part Request System

When you need something not in standard inventory, field workers submit custom requests with urgency levels. Full history and status tracking eliminates lost requests.
